Take charge of your study life, achieve your study goal and feel in control.

Achievy suggests the minimum amount to study each day and visualizes what you will achieve on the deadline.

Features:

  • Let you create your own material to study - be it your book, online video course, etc.
  • Suggests minimum amount to study each day.
  • Tells you what the "new" tomorrow amount will be if you don't study today. With this, you have the flexibility to adjust your plan as life happens.

Great for:

  • Preparing for exams that require you to study a lot of materials in a limited time.
  • Self-study and make sure you are in control of your goal.
