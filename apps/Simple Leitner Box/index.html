Simple Leitner Box

Technologies

  • CloudKit
  • Simple Unit Testing

This app was born on a night which I had to prepare for a few heavily memorization-based exams. It is based on the Leitner System. This system was proposed by Sebastian Leitner in the 1970s.

The idea of this is that in order to remember something, you have to review it at a certain interval, also known as "the forgetting curve". To combat this, Spaced-Repetition methods were invented.

Today, there are many variations of SRS implementation, integration, and software such as Anki, Rosetta Stone etc.

This app is based on the simple 7-levels, 64-days cycle schedule. Basically, you prepare a box by putting it in 7 sections (levels). Then you move the cards up or down the levels. Those levels are due on different days depend on the interval. Learn more about this on Wikipedia.

The problem is that creating a shedule is time consuming, and if you missed a day or forgot to track, you lose the mess up the schedule. That's why this app was made.
